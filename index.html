Privacy Policy for NavNiti

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

NavNiti ("the Application") is built and operated by UshaVi Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") as a local-first, privacy-driven utility. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you of our strict practices regarding the protection, non-collection, and non-sharing of your personal and financial data.

By utilizing NavNiti, you acknowledge and agree to the architecture outlined in this policy.

1. Core Philosophy: Zero Data Collection

Our fundamental architectural choice is that your data belongs to you.

  • We do not operate remote database servers.
  • We do not collect, monitor, store, or transmit your personal identity, financial balances, mutual fund portfolios, or transaction logs.
  • We do not display third-party advertisements or integrate tracking SDKs.

2. On-Device Local Processing

All data processing occurs strictly inside your physical mobile device.

  • CAS Statement Parsing: When you choose to import a Consolidated Account Statement (CAS) PDF or Excel file from CAMS or KFintech, the parsing engine extracts textual transaction blocks entirely on your local CPU.
  • Local Storage: The extracted financial records, multi-profile allocations, and custom drift calculations are saved exclusively inside an isolated, secure on-device database wrapper (SQLite).

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

Because we do not collect or store any data, it is physically impossible for us to share, sell, distribute, or monetize your information with any third-party corporate entities, advertising networks, or data brokers.

4. Device Permissions

The Application may request specific device-level access tokens to execute localized tasks:

  • Storage / File Access: Explicitly required to let you select and import your downloaded CAS statements into the local database engine.

Note: These permissions are utilized entirely in-memory on your device; no file contents are ever uploaded externally.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may periodically update our Privacy Policy to align with new application capabilities or changing regulatory frameworks. Any changes will be reflected directly on this hosted URL with an updated revision date.
